Darkhan Juzz

The star of qazaq indie - trap performer Darkhan Juzz began his creative career on the Arbat, and one of the best hits “Sheker” composed in the back room of the restaurant, where he worked as a waiter. Aksai boy from the district, Darkhan literally grew up with a guitar in his hands. According to him, he played at least six hours a day after school and another hour in the morning. Then he began to compose songs. Honest lyrics and an unusual, original style quickly found their listeners. Darkhan was not shown on TV or played on the radio, but thousands of people on Vkontakte added his songs and downloaded the same number to their phones. “I grew up on the streets of Aksai and after finishing 9th grade I entered the profession of a designer in college, which I dropped out after a year and a half. I have neither musical nor any other profession. I don't even know the notes, but I know and love music. "

Moldanazar

Galymzhan was born in Kyzylozek, in the 10th grade he began to write songs, then ended up in Kyzylorda, studied at the Zhurgenov Kazakh National Academy of Arts in Almaty. For the first time the local audience saw him at the music symposium of the Hit TV channel in May of the same year, where he sang the song “Жылама жүрегім” with the rock star Asem. He became widely known to the public in 2013, after the clip "Aқпен бірге". In 2015 he founded the Moldanazar group. In the same year, the American rock cellist group Break of Reality performed in Almaty on the stage of the Zhambyl State Philharmonic Society, where they met Galymzhan. After that Moldanazar and Break of Reality performed together at one of the New York venues. The concert was also attended by Kazakhstanis living in the USA. Another video for "Ақпен Бірге" was shot there.

Eminem

Eminem was born on October 17, 1972 in St. Joseph, Missouri. Father left the family when Marshall was not even a year old. Then the family settled in Detroit, but even there it was not easy for Marshall - he often changed schools, got into fights. Carried away by rap, Marshall came up with songs at the age of 13, and at 15 he founded his own group. After graduating from high school, Eminem tried several low-paid jobs, performed on the radio. The next year, in the biography of Marshall Mathers, the first album "Infinite" was released, which did not become particularly successful. Having started to cooperate with Dr. Dre, Eminem has released "Slim Shady LP". Then he signed a contract with Aftermath Entertainment. In 2001, Eminem's biography received three Grammy awards. The singer also became a member of the group D12. In 2002 the album "The Eminem Show" was released, in 2004 - "Encore". In 2001 he played in the film "The Wash". The film "8 Mile" achieved great success in 2002, and for the song "Lose Yourself" Eminem received an Oscar.

Lorde

New Zealand singer, songwriter and music producer. Her debut single "Royals", released in March 2013 and winning the Grammy Award for Best Song of the Year, became a worldwide hit, topping the national charts in New Zealand, Italy, Great Britain. as well as the Billboard Hot 100 chart, making Lord the only New Zealand performer with such an achievement. Their debut album, Pure Heroine, featuring producer Joel Little, was released on September 27, 2013, to a critical acclaim and debuted at number three on the Billboard 200. In 2014, Lord recorded the song "Yellow Flicker Beat", which was released as a single to the soundtrack The Hunger Games: Mockingjay. In June 2017, Lord's second studio album, Melodrama, was released. The disc is the most critically acclaimed performer's album for 2017. Singles such as "Green Light" and "Perfect Places" were released in support of the disc.

Lana Del Rey

Lana Del Rey is an American singer who won the love of listeners with her biographical songs with a dark aesthetic. Bribing sincerity is intertwined with obscene vocabulary, and light energy - with daring sexuality. In January 2010, the album “Lana Del Ray a.k.a. Lizzy Grant ". At the end of 2011, the singer presented the single "Born to Die", anticipating the release of the album of the same name, which took the fifth position among the best-selling albums of the year. Critics have called it a link between modern hip-hop culture and classical pop music, equipped with jazz elements. In 2014, her new album "Ultraviolence" is released, which Del Rey calls "conceptual" and the genre refers to "psychedelic rock". A year later, the singer delighted the audience with her new album "Honeymoon". In 2017, the album "Lust For Life" was released, in which the rapper The Weeknd, ASAP Rocky also took part.
